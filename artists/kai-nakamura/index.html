Kai Nakamura

Photography · Film
Berkeley, CA

Kai Nakamura works at the intersection of documentary photography and fine art, using medium-format film to capture the atmospheric tension between urban infrastructure and natural light. His Bay Area series examines fog, shadow, and the slow erosion of industrial landscapes.
