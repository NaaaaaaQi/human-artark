James Park

Digital Art · New Media
San Jose, CA

James Park writes code as an artistic act. His generative works produce visual systems that evolve in real time — grids that degrade, color fields that drift, typographies that collapse. Trained as both a software engineer and an artist, he works in the gap between tool and expression.
