Sofia Reyes

Painting · Drawing
San Francisco, CA · Mission District

Sofia Reyes paints figures in states of suspension — bodies caught between gesture and stillness, rendered in warm terracotta and charcoal. Her work is rooted in the Mission District community where she grew up, translating personal and collective memory into layered oil paintings.
