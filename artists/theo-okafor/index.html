Theo Okafor

Video Art · Performance
Oakland, CA

Theo Okafor's practice moves fluidly between video, live performance, and sound. His works explore Black masculinity, ritual, and the transformation of public space through durational acts. Often staged in unexpected urban environments, his performances leave traces — video stills, objects, marks — that become the gallery work.
