Amara Diallo

Sculpture · Installation
Oakland, CA

Amara Diallo creates large-scale installations that negotiate memory, material, and the politics of space. Drawing from West African textile traditions and industrial salvage, her work transforms galleries into environments that demand physical navigation and emotional reckoning.
