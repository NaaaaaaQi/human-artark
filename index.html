Our mission

Every year, thousands of artists in the Bay Area create work of genuine consequence — and most galleries never hear from them. Human ArtArk is the infrastructure that closes that gap: a curated database of galleries, a professional profile for every artist, and a direct channel to the people who decide what gets shown.

Step 01

Build your artist profile

Upload your works, write your statement, add your CV and exhibition history. Your profile becomes a permanent, shareable page — the professional record galleries expect before they say yes.

Step 02

Find galleries with open calls

Our database covers 70+ Bay Area galleries — updated weekly by scraping websites and parsing newsletters. See exactly who is accepting submissions, what they're looking for, and when the deadline is.

Step 03

Apply with one click

Select a gallery, write a short note, and send. Your profile is attached automatically. We handle the outreach on your behalf and track the status of every application in one place.

Profile. Every artist on Human ArtArk has one permanent, gallery-ready page that travels with every application.

The art world has always had gatekeepers. That is not the problem. The problem is opacity — artists who don't know which doors to knock on, galleries that miss the work they would have shown if only someone had made the introduction.

Human ArtArk is not a marketplace. It is not a social network. It is infrastructure: a clear, maintained record of who is showing what, who is accepting submissions, and what a rigorous artist profile looks like. The rest is up to the work.

We exist because human-made art matters — not as a category, but as the evidence of a specific consciousness at a specific moment in time. That is irreplaceable. And it deserves to be seen.
