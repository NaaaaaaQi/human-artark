How it works

From studio to gallery,
in three steps.

No cold emails. No guesswork. A structured path for emerging artists to connect with galleries.

01
Build your profile
Create a standardized artist profile with your works, statement, CV, and contact — ready to send to any gallery.
02
Find open calls
Our database tracks 70+ Bay Area galleries, updated weekly. See which galleries are actively accepting submissions.
03
Apply with one click
Send a professional inquiry to any gallery — your profile is automatically attached. We manage the outreach.
04
Track your applications
See the status of every application in one place. Know when galleries have opened new calls or responded.
