How it works

01 — Profile

Build your artist profile

Upload works, write your statement, add CV and exhibition history. Your profile is a permanent, shareable page — the professional record galleries expect before they say yes.

02 — Discover

Find open calls & residencies

Our database covers galleries and residencies globally — updated continuously. Filter by medium, region, deadline, and type to find the right fit.

03 — Apply

Apply with one click

Select a gallery or residency, write a short note, and send. Your profile is attached automatically. Track every application in one dashboard.

04 — Sell

Sell works directly

List works for sale on your profile. Collectors discover and purchase directly — no gallery commission, no intermediary. You keep 100% of what you earn.

The art world has always had gatekeepers. That is not the problem. The problem is opacity — artists who don't know which doors to knock on, institutions that miss the work they would have championed if only someone had made the introduction.

Human ArtArk is infrastructure. A maintained record of who is showing what, who is accepting submissions, where residencies are opening, and what a rigorous artist profile looks like. We do not curate. We connect.

We exist because human-made art matters — not as a category, but as the irreplaceable evidence of a specific consciousness at a specific moment in time. That deserves to be seen. Everywhere.
